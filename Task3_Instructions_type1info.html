Part 2: Two Types of Information to Help You Guess the true value of Ө
You will receive two types of information that will help you guess the true value of Ө. See details below.

Type 1: Color Information
Test Scores: The 10 questions you just completed before this instruction are often used as non-verbal tests of intelligence. Scores on these IQ questions are ranked among today’s participants from highest (top, 1st) to lowest (bottom, 8th). Tie scores are broken randomly.

Color Marking: At the beginning of each round, the computer first randomly sorts all 8 traders into 4 pairs (2 traders each pair). In each pair, the trader with a higher (resp. lower) rank on the ten IQ questions is assigned to the H (resp. L) group. Then, the computer will randomly determine the true value of Ө. As shown in Figure 1, when the state is G, the H-group traders will be marked as Green and L-group traders will be marked as Red; the marking will be reversed if the state is B.

You will NOT know whether you were assigned to the H or L group. But you will be told your group’s color (this is your color information) after the true value of Ө is determined. Your color may help you guess how likely the true state is G.
