Part 3: Survey Pages For Payment

In each round, you need to answer two questions multiple times. The more accurate your answers, the more extra payment you will receive. The number of times you need to answer the survey questions depends on the number of private signal \(s\) you receive in each round.
You will play 18 - 24 rounds in today’s session. We randomly pick half of the rounds to send you one private signal, \(s\); and in the other half rounds, you will receive two private signals, which are independent from each other.
Please note that once the accuracy of the private signal (\(var_{s}\)) is determined for a round, the private signal \(s\) is drawn from the same orange distribution, regardless of whether you receive one or two signals.
The questions are as follows.
Question 1: Please make a guess of the true value of the asset in this round. Please enter a number that reflects your belief; if you think that, given the current information, the true value of \(\theta\) is 65, then type 65 into the answer box.
Question 2: Please make a guess of the accuracy of your private signal in this round. (A value between 0.25 and 4) Given the current information, you need to guess the accuracy, i.e., \(var_{s}\) of your private signal, \(s\), in this round.

The first survey comes right after the computer shows you your color, green or red. You need to answer question 1 after receiving your color. Please see the User Interface below.

Then, click the “Next” button to go to the second survey.
